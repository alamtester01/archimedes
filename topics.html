Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn like humans. Some AI topics include:

  • Machine Learning
  • Deep Learning
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Computer Vision
  • Robotics
  • Expert Systems
  • Neural Networks

These are just a few examples of the vast and exciting field of AI. The advancements in AI have the potential to revolutionize various industries and improve our daily lives.
